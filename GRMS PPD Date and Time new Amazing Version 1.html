📝 Filing

How to File a Grievance

1
Click File Grievance in the top navigation
2
Choose a Category and set the Priority
3
Fill in Subject, Location, and a detailed Description
4
Attach supporting evidence — photos, PDFs, videos
5
Click Submit — your unique Tracking ID is issued instantly

Attaching evidence makes your grievance 3× more likely to be resolved quickly. Drag & drop or click to upload.

Track Your Grievance

Every submission gets a unique Tracking ID (e.g. GRM-2024-001). Use it to follow each step in real-time.

●
Open — Received, awaiting acknowledgement
●
In Progress — Under active investigation
●
Resolved — Issue addressed successfully
●
Rejected — Not accepted (you may appeal)

🚨 Safety

Ragging & Harassment

Ragging is a serious offense under UGC Anti-Ragging Regulations 2009. GRMS ensures immediate priority handling.

1
Select Ragging / Harassment category
2
Set priority to Critical
3
Describe incident with date, time, names
4
Attach photos or witness statements
Your identity is fully protected. Submit without fear. 🙏
